FUTURE_DS_02

PowerBI dashboard for analyzing  churn and key retention drivers

---

PROJECT OVERVIEW


This project aims to help business  understand why customers are leaving the platform, and suggest actionable strategies to improve retention. 

---
OBJECTIVES


The aim of the project was to find to out the following;

1)Why are customers leaving the platform?

2)Which customer segments are most likely to churn?

3)How long do customers typically stay active ?

4)What actions can improve customer retention?

---

DATA SET DESCRIPTION


The data set contained the following information;

a)Customers who left within the last month – the column is called Churn

b)Services that each customer has signed up for – phone, multiple lines, internet, online security, online backup, device protection, tech support, and streaming TV and movies

c)Customer account information – how long they’ve been a customer, contract, payment method, paperless billing, monthly charges, and total charges

d)Demographic info about customers – gender, age range, and if they have partners and dependents

---

TOOLS AND TECHNIQUES USED


1)Power BI for data visualization and dashboard creation.

2)Power Query for cleaning and transforming data.

3)DAX(Data anlysis expression ) for creating measures like churn rate.

KEY INSIGHTS


1)A significant portion of churn occurs in the very first month of service. This could be because the services did not meet their expectation or they found it expensive.

2)Customers on Month-to-month contracts are  more likely to churn compared to those on one or two-year contracts.

3)Customers  paying via Electronic Check have a  higher churn rate than those using automated bank transfers or credit cards.This could be because for the electronic check the customer has to update it manually and sometimes it is tiresome.

4)Customers with Dependents or Partners  tend to stay longer than those without.

5)Non-seniors are more likely to churn compared to seniors.

---

RECCOMENDATIONS

1)Improve onboarding experience for new customers.

2)Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts by offering a free month subscription for all the services.

3)Promote automatic payment methods maybe by offering cash refund for all those using electronic check that move to either aoutomatic bank transfer or credit card.

4)Offer different and more less expensive packages to customers with dependants or partners.
